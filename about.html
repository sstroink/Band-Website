About the band

Spider of the Embodied Guava is a experimental alternative rock group based out of Victoria, BC. Band members consist of bassist Reid, lead singer Sam, guitarist Rosco and Sonny Mcintosh on keyboard.

Their first show was at an art gallery in Victoria in 1980. The police came and described the scene as “completly horrifying.” The police actually ended up burning the entire building to the ground because they were so afraid of everythingthat they saw. Reid says the show could not have gone better, “Yes, it was horrifying. But that’s how we like it.” SotEG often gets asked how they came up with their ricidulous band name. The answer is simple, from a band name generating website .
